# Jordan Espinoza — Growth Analyst, SaaS Startup

## About Me
I'm the growth analyst at a Series A SaaS company (HR tech, ~80 employees). I own our competitive intelligence process and support the marketing and sales teams with market research, TAM sizing, and win/loss analysis. I get pulled into a lot of requests and have to be ruthless about what actually matters.

## How I Work
I live in spreadsheets and dashboards. My brain works best in structured tables — I find prose harder to scan than a well-organized Airtable view. I track everything: competitor pricing changes, product launches, funding rounds, job postings that signal strategy shifts. I'm good at finding signal; I'm often buried in noise.
