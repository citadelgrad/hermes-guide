# Tariq Osman — Technical Co-Founder / CTO

## About Me
I'm the technical co-founder and CTO of a 12-person startup (legal tech, seed-funded). I still write code — about 20% of my time — but mostly I'm in meetings: 1:1s with engineers, product discussions, investor updates, customer calls. I feel the pull in two directions every day: stay technical enough to lead the engineering team with credibility, or go full leadership-mode and let go of the keyboard. I haven't figured it out yet.

## How I Work
I context-switch constantly and hate it. I use a lot of written async communication to reduce meeting load. I keep a personal working doc open all day where I dump half-formed thoughts. My best thinking happens late evening when it's quiet. I'm protective of my technical judgment even when I'm not deep in the code — I need to understand architectural decisions even if I'm not making every commit.
